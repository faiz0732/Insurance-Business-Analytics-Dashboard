# 🛡️ INSURANCE BUSINESS ANALYTICS DASHBOARD

An end-to-end insurance analytics project using **SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python** to analyze revenue, sales performance, pipeline efficiency, and opportunity management.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **insurance business operations** using multiple datasets such as opportunities, brokerage, fees, invoices, meetings, and targets.

The goal is to provide insights into:

- Revenue generation and financial performance  
- Sales pipeline and conversion efficiency  
- Account executive performance  
- Client engagement and activity tracking  
- High-value opportunities  

The project demonstrates a **complete analytics lifecycle**:

- Raw data ingestion  
- Data cleaning and preparation (Excel)  
- SQL-based data modeling and KPI creation  
- Excel dashboard development  
- Power BI interactive dashboards  
- Business insights and storytelling  

---

## 💡 Key Findings & Business Insights

### 🔍 Key Findings

1. **Strong Revenue Performance:** Total revenue reached approximately **$33.89M**.  
2. **Low Conversion Rate:** Only ~**10.2%** of opportunities reach final stage.  
3. **Pipeline Bottleneck:** Majority of opportunities are stuck in the **Qualify stage**.  
4. **Performance Imbalance:** A few account executives contribute most of the revenue.  
5. **Overperformance Cases:** Some executives significantly exceed their targets.  
6. **Engagement Gap:** Number of meetings is relatively low compared to opportunities.  

---

### 📌 Actionable Recommendations

1. Improve early-stage conversion (Qualify → Propose).  
2. Increase focus on deal closure strategies.  
3. Balance workload among account executives.  
4. Increase client engagement through meetings.  
5. Optimize target allocation for realistic planning.  

---

## 🔍 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description | Business Impact |
|-----|------------|----------------|
| Total Revenue | Brokerage + Fees | Financial performance |
| Total Opportunities | Number of opportunities | Pipeline strength |
| Conversion Rate | Closed ÷ Total opportunities | Sales efficiency |
| Total Invoices | Number of invoices | Operational activity |
| Total Meetings | Client meetings count | Engagement level |
| Avg Revenue per Opportunity | Revenue ÷ Opportunities | Deal quality |
